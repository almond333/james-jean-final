NOTES FROM THE ARTIST:

The Dowser traverses the chrysanthemum, seekingnectar. The birds expose the flower's equator, revealing its aromatic syrups. In a prismatic celebration of Spring and its arrival, the birds and flower glisten with sculptural details, foiled linework, and dimensionally embellished drips and specks. A portion of the proceeds have been donated to the AAPI Community Fund.
